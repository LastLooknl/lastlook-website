About us

Find out more about LastLook!

Mission

Our mission is to offer a seamlessly accessible platform that facilitates the exchange of second-hand costumes, while cultivating a vibrant community where every theatre performer and creator is empowered to unleash their imagination.

Vision

Our vision is to create a world where theatre performers and creators have the ultimate creative freedom. Through our platform, we aim to foster a global community with an upcycling mentality that celebrates imagination, collaboration, and inclusivity.





Circular movement

Like a lot of aspects in life, we need each other, sustainability is no exception. Sustainability has more impact together. LastLook strives to give costumes an endless life, using them again and again. This is done by letting them go from person to person letting them move in a circular motion, experiencing many different lives.

Community cultivation

Through a welcoming environment we want to promote and create collaboration with people sharing the same passion. This is done through creating a supporting network where everyone can thrive and use their own skills and qualities. Sharing a passion while using different qualities is the perfect recipe for a functioning community.

Empowerment of creativity

We believe people should have the ultimate creative freedom, nothing should be in the way of this. We want people to take control of their own journey and do things the way that they want. Creativity should have no limits or barriers.

Inclusive access

LastLook believes that everyone deserves the same opportunities meaning equality. Easy access contributes to this greatly, as easy access allows everyone to have the same opportunities. We want to limit any barriers as much as possible, allowing everyone to express their creativity.
