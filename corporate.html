Context

LastLook

LastLook has embarked on a groundbreaking campaign aimed at changing the world of theatre by attending to a community that stands for creativity, sustainability, and inclusivity. We envision a world where theatre performers and creators have the ultimate creative freedom to express themselves without limitations. Our platform serves as a beacon for theatre enthusiasts, offering a seamless and accessible solution for the exchange of second-hand costumes. With Last Look, we aim to create a global community where every theatre enthusiast can unleash their imagination, celebrate collaboration, and contribute to a more sustainable future for the performing arts.

Through our platform, we aspire to provide a marketplace for the buying and selling of costumes that also creates connections within the theatre community. By facilitating the exchange of pre-loved costumes, we aim to reduce waste and promote a culture of upcycling, where every garment finds new life on the stage.

Our target demographic comprises 18 to 25-year-old (theatre) students struggling with the challenges of sourcing and disposing of costumes. To gain insights into their social media habits, we conducted a survey. This survey (Microsoft Forms - Free Tool to Create Online Surveys, Forms, Polls, and Quizzes, n.d.) questioned their platform preferences, usage duration, timing, location, and intended purposes. Findings indicate that Instagram and TikTok are the most used platforms, making them optimal channels for promotional activities. On these platforms, we made relatable, attention-grabbing, and humorous content by tapping into current trends. For instance, we shared memes on Instagram and recreated popular trends on TikTok. According to Moursi (2023), “Gen Z wants to see authentic, funny, and relatable content. Some values that resonate with Gen Z are authenticity, connection, community, and self-expression.” This means that the type of content we created is perfect for the target group. Our values also align with theirs, which makes our brand ideal for them. In addition to these posts, we also featured more informative content on Instagram. For example, the explanation of the buying and selling process. Our primary objective with all of these posts was to stay relevant to our target audience and blend in with their existing interests.

Objectives

Instagram Reach: When looking at our previous short film marketing campaign, a new Instagram account can have a reach of up to 500 accounts. This means that 500 Instagram accounts saw our posts and stories and are aware of our brand. Therefore our objective is: by the first of April 2024 we want to have 500 accounts reached by posting posts to create brand awareness. Response: When looking at the conversion rates and after looking at the marketing results of our previous short film project, we think it is reasonable to receive 10 likes and 2 comments. Our response objective is, reaching 500 accounts, receiving 10 likes and 2 comments per post by the first of April 2024. Tiktok Reach Based on projects from the previous year and from looking at other accounts we have decided that we want to reach 800 accounts. This means that by the first of April 2024 we want to have 800 accounts reached by posting posts to create brand awareness. Response When looking at the marketing results we did on other projects, after looking at other peoples accounts from last year and other years, and based on our own predictions we did through looking at other small accounts on tiktok, our response objective is to receive around 15 likes per post, 2 comments and 1 save.
